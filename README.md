# ORCA Technical Steering Committee (TSC)

The ORCA Technical Steering Committee (TSC) is responsible for oversight of the
technical initiatives of ORCA.

## Get Involved

Although the TSC is composed of a set of official members listed below, any
community member is welcome to participate in TSC discussions.

Official communications occur on the [TSC mailing list](https://lists.orca-lf.org/g/orca-tsc/topics). [Manage your subscriptions to ORCA mailing lists](https://lists.orca-lf.org/g/main/subgroups).

Informal discussions occur in the TSC channel of the [ORCA Slack](https://orca-lf.slack.com).
To join, use the following [invite link](https://join.slack.com/t/orca-lf/shared_invite/zt-3jlxetgj4-n4nKI0sG1JJM7p~5AMu3Zw).

Use [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/ORCA-LF/tsc/issues) to request and discuss agenda items.

## Meetings

The TSC [meetings minutes]() are online and appear on the [ORCA Community Calendar]().

Meetings are also recorded and shared on the main ORCA mailing list.
